Interactive Thyroid Disease Prediction

This tool uses machine learning style symptom scoring to predict potential thyroid disorders based on your health indicators. It is meant for educational screening only and is not a medical diagnosis. Always consult a healthcare professional for testing, diagnosis, and treatment.

Health Assessment

Adjust the indicators below to generate a symptom-based thyroid risk estimate and recommended next steps.

0 = none, 10 = severe exhaustion

0 = weight gain, 10 = weight loss

0 = heat sensitive, 10 = cold sensitive

0 = none, 10 = severe mood impact

0 = slow pulse, 10 = rapid pulse

0 = none, 10 = severe concentration problems

What it checksFatigue, weight shifts, temperature sensitivity, mood, heart rate, skin, hair, and focus.
Possible outcomesHypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, or low-risk thyroid function patterns.
Best next stepUse the result as a screening prompt, then confirm with thyroid lab testing.

Adjust your symptoms in the form and click Get Prediction to see your personalized analysis.

Your prediction will appear here with a risk label, explanation, and recommended medical follow-up steps.
